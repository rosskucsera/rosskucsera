Product Pages

Overview

The individual PeopleMatter product pages had become overly focused on features. Product marketing decided to focus more on highlighting the value of each product instead of individual features.

Problem

Current product pages use a significant portion of the screen real estate to highlight individual features. The design and flow of content do not tell a story about the product's value.

Solution

Consolidated all of the information previously on the product pages the individual product pages. Put this new content in skimmable sections with simple navigation that highlighted value-based messaging.

Skills used

  • Content
  • Planning/Wireframing
  • Design
  • HTML/CSS

To help users better understand the value of each product. We reused this tabbed slider that we had designed for the value proposition page. It allowed us to drastically reduce the height of our pages while grouping content for easier scanability.

In the printed marketing collateral we had developed this simple workflow for each product. It was one of the initial block types we created in the pattern library project and placed on each product page.

The feature grid is something we originally design for our printed collateral. We used the Font Awesome icons we'd recently added to the site to keep things consistent and make it easier for a user to identify with what exactly the feature did. To stay true to our original goal of not overemphasising features we created a 'see more' button and limited the original amount to only six of the most important ones.
