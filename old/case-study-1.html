Website Pattern Library and 2014 Redesign

The corporate website at PeopleMatter has gone through three major revisions since 2010. An entirely new set of product marketing collateral had been created in the months leading up to the redesign that needed to be reflected on the website.

This redesign was a chance to put in a solid foundation for more rapid and consistent development in the future.
