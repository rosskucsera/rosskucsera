PeopleMatter Value Proposition

Overview

The product marketing and sales teams wanted to develop a set of sales collateral that highlighted the value of PeopleMatter products versus going heavy on features. Along with sales presentations and printed collateral, the Results Page as it came to be known described in detail PeopleMatter's five main value propositions.

Problem

All website content at this time was heavily feature-focused. Sales team gave the feedback that while features are important, they ended up getting into "feature battles" with competitors. To help the sales team, collateral and webpages needed to be created to help differentiate PeopleMatter.

Solution

Created a page that could be used by website visitors as well as sales team members during presentations to easily explain the company's value proposition.
